Logistics

Getting to Winston-Salem

Winston-Salem is served primarily by the Piedmont Triad International Airport (GSO) in Greensboro, NC (30 minutes from downtown Winston-Salem). Taxis and rental cars are available, and express shuttle service is operated from the PTI Airport to Winston-Salem. For more information you can visit visitwinstonsalem.com.

Other regional airports include:

Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT) is about 90 minutes away. Information on transportation to Winston-Salem from CLT can be found at the airport website or you may want to check out a company called Official Shuttle with transfers to Winston-Salem.

Raleigh/Durham International Airport (RDU) is about a 2-hour drive away. Information on transportation to Winston-Salem from RDU can be found at the airport website.

NOTE: Smith Reynolds Airport (INT) in Winston-Salem is available primarily to charter and private aircraft.
